## Content Workflow Overview

The MMS PPG website uses a structured workflow to ensure all published content meets quality standards and maintains consistency. This process involves multiple stages and user roles working together to create, review, and publish content.

<img src="/images/content/workflow-overview.png" alt="Content workflow diagram showing the progression from draft to published" />

## Workflow Stages

### Stage 1: Draft

<Card title="Draft Stage" icon="edit">
**Purpose**: Content creation and initial development

**Who's Involved**: Content Creators

**What Happens**:
- Content is created and saved as "Draft"
- Only the creator and administrators can see it
- Unlimited edits and revisions allowed
- No time pressure or deadlines
- Content remains private and unpublished

**Duration**: As long as needed
</Card>

**Best Practices for Draft Stage:**

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="âœï¸ Content Planning">
    **Before You Start Writing:**
    - Define your target audience
    - Identify the main message or purpose
    - Research any facts or statistics you'll include
    - Check if similar content already exists
    - Plan your content structure with headings
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="ðŸ“ Writing Process">
    **While Creating Content:**
    - Save your work frequently (Ctrl+S)
    - Use clear, descriptive headings
    - Write in plain English
    - Include relevant keywords naturally
    - Add placeholder text for images you'll need
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="ðŸ” Self-Review Checklist">
    **Before Submitting for Review:**
    - [ ] Spelling and grammar checked
    - [ ] All required fields completed
    - [ ] Images added with alt text
    - [ ] Links tested and working
    - [ ] Content follows PPG style guidelines
    - [ ] Accessibility considerations addressed
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Stage 2: In Review

<Card title="In Review Stage" icon="search">
**Purpose**: Quality assurance and content approval

**Who's Involved**: Content Reviewers

**What Happens**:
- Content Creator changes status to "In Review"
- Reviewers are automatically notified
- Content remains private during review
- Reviewers can add comments and suggestions
- Content may be approved or sent back for changes

**Duration**: Typically 2-3 business days
</Card>

**Review Process Details:**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Automatic Notification">
    When content is submitted for review:
    - All Content Reviewers receive email notification
    - Dashboard shows new items awaiting review
    - Review deadline is set (usually 3 business days)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Reviewer Assessment">
    Reviewers check content for:
    - **Accuracy**: Facts, dates, and information correctness
    - **Clarity**: Easy to understand language and structure
    - **Consistency**: Follows PPG style and tone guidelines
    - **Completeness**: All necessary information included
    - **Accessibility**: Proper headings, alt text, and structure
    - **Legal/Compliance**: Meets healthcare communication standards
  </Step>

  <Step title="Reviewer Actions">
    Reviewers can:
    - **Approve**: Content is ready for publication
    - **Request Changes**: Send back with specific feedback
    - **Reject**: Content needs major revision or is inappropriate
    - **Add Comments**: Provide suggestions and guidance
  </Step>

  <Step title="Feedback Communication">
    - Comments are added directly to the content item
    - Email notifications sent to content creator
    - Specific suggestions for improvement provided
    - Deadline for revisions communicated
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Stage 3: Approved

<Card title="Approved Stage" icon="check-circle">
**Purpose**: Content ready for publication

**Who's Involved**: Content Publishers

**What Happens**:
- Content has passed review process
- Ready for publication scheduling
- May be published immediately or scheduled
- Final quality check before going live

**Duration**: Usually published within 24 hours
</Card>

**Publication Process:**

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Immediate Publication">
    **When to Use:**
    - Time-sensitive announcements
    - Urgent health information
    - Breaking news or updates
    - Event reminders
    
    **Process:**
    1. Publisher reviews approved content
    2. Checks publication date and time
    3. Changes status to "Published"
    4. Content goes live immediately
    5. Social media and newsletter promotion (if applicable)
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Scheduled Publication">
    **When to Use:**
    - Regular blog posts
    - Planned announcements
    - Coordinated campaigns
    - Optimal timing for audience
    
    **Process:**
    1. Publisher sets publication date/time
    2. Content remains in "Approved" status
    3. Automatic publication at scheduled time
    4. Notifications sent when published
    5. Analytics tracking begins
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Stage 4: Published

<Card title="Published Stage" icon="globe">
**Purpose**: Content is live and accessible to the public

**Who's Involved**: All website visitors

**What Happens**:
- Content appears on the website
- Search engines can index the content
- Analytics tracking begins
- Social sharing becomes available
- Comments may be enabled (if applicable)

**Duration**: Indefinite (until archived or updated)
</Card>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="ðŸ‘ï¸ Responsibilities">
    **Primary Tasks:**
    - Review submitted content for quality and accuracy

</AccordionGroup>

<AccordionGroup>

    - Social media scheduling
    - Newsletter inclusion
    - Homepage featuring
    - Cross-linking with related content
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

    - Review deadline approaching

### Automated Actions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Status Change Triggers">
    **When content status changes:**
    - Automatic email notifications sent
    - Dashboard counters updated
    - Workflow timers started
    - Analytics tracking begins/ends
  </Step>

  <Step title="Scheduled Publishing">
    **For scheduled content:**
    - Automatic status change at specified time
    - Website cache invalidation
    - Search engine notification
    - Social media posting (if configured)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Content Archiving">
    **For outdated content:**
    - Automatic archiving of old events
    - Notification to content creators
    - Redirect setup for moved content
    - Analytics data preservation

## Troubleshooting Workflow Issues

### Common Problems and Solutions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="ðŸŒ Slow Review Process">
    **Symptoms:**
    - Content stuck in review for days
    - Reviewers not responding
    - Missed publication deadlines

    - Set up automatic reminder notifications

    - Establish clear review deadlines

    - Regular reviewer training and feedback

  <Accordion title="ðŸ”„ Frequent Rejections">

    - Implement pre-submission checklists
    - Regular feedback sessions
    - Template and example library
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="ðŸ“… Publication Delays">

## Emergency Content Procedures

### Urgent Content Publication

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="ðŸš¨ Emergency Situations">

    - Practice closure announcements
    - Health and safety alerts
    - System outages or technical issues
    - Urgent policy changes
    - Emergency contact information updates
    
    **Emergency Workflow:**
    1. Content Creator creates urgent content
    2. Immediate notification to designated reviewer
    3. Expedited review (within 2 hours)
    4. Direct publication by authorized publisher
    5. Post-publication quality check
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="âš¡ Fast-Track Process">

    **Fast-Track Workflow:**
    1. Content marked as "Urgent" in system
    2. Automatic priority notification sent
    3. Same-day review requirement
    4. Publication within 4 hours of approval

  </Accordion>
