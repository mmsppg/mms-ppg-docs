## What is the MMS PPG Website?

The **MMS PPG (Patient Participation Group) Website** is a modern, accessible web platform designed to serve the healthcare community. Built with cutting-edge technology and a focus on accessibility, it provides a seamless experience for patients, healthcare providers, and community members.

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose Your Role">
    Identify your role in the content management process:
    - **Content Creator**: Create and edit content
    - **Content Reviewer**: Review and approve content
    - **Content Publisher**: Publish and manage content
    - **Administrator**: Full system access
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Access the Admin Panel">
    Navigate to the [Admin Panel](https://mmsppg.themainhost.co.uk/admin) and log in with your credentials.
    
    <img src="/images/login-screen.png" alt="Directus login screen" />
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Explore the Dashboard">
    Familiarize yourself with the dashboard layout and available features.
    
    <img src="/images/dashboard-overview.png" alt="Admin dashboard overview" />
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start Creating Content">
    Begin creating your first piece of content following our guidelines.
  </Step>
</Steps>
